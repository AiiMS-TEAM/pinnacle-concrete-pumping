Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the things customers ask us most often.

How fast can you get a pump to my site?expand_more

For most metropolitan Sydney locations, we can have a pump on site the same day if you book before mid-morning. Outside business hours, we'll respond first thing the next morning.

What's the difference between a line pump and a boom pump?expand_more

A line pump uses flexible hoses - perfect for slabs, footings and tight-access jobs. A boom pump uses a hydraulic arm mounted on a truck - best for upper-level pours, large slabs and hard-to-reach pour points. Not sure which one you need? Just ask.

Which suburbs do you service?expand_more

We service all of greater Sydney - from the Inner West to Western Sydney, the Hills, Eastern Suburbs, North Shore, Sutherland Shire and beyond. If you're not sure, give us a call.

How much does concrete pumping cost?expand_more

Pricing depends on pump type, job size, access and duration. We provide free, no-obligation quotes - usually within 30 minutes during business hours. New customers get 10% off their first booking.

Can you handle large commercial pours?expand_more

Absolutely. We work regularly with builders, developers and civil contractors on commercial and industrial pours. We can also offer discounted rates for recurring and scheduled work.

Are you licensed and insured?expand_more

Yes - every operator is ticketed, and we carry full public liability and workers compensation insurance. Documentation is available on request.
